About RK’s JustLED

Innovative LED Lighting Solutions for Modern Infrastructure

RK JustLED Company is a leading provider of modern LED lighting solutions, serving clients across Bangalore and India. The company specializes in designing energy-efficient, durable, and aesthetically appealing lighting products.

With a strong commitment to innovation and quality, RK JustLED manufactures a wide range of products including street light poles, garden lighting, architectural poles, and indoor LED fixtures.

Our mission is to deliver eco-friendly, cost-effective, and high-performance lighting solutions that enhance urban and commercial spaces.
